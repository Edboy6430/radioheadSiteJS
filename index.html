Radiohead are an English rock band formed in 1985 and have been making music for over 35 years. The band consists of Thom Yorke (vocals, guitar, piano, keyboards), brothers Jonny (lead guitar, keyboards, other instruments) and Colin Greenwood (bass), and Ed O'Brien (guitar, backing vocals) and Philip Selway (drums, percussion).


Some of their accomplishments:

  • Six (6) Grammies and four (4) Ivor Novello Awards
  • Named by Rolling Stones as one of the 100 greatest artists of all time
  • Inducted into the Rock Hall of Fame

Why I love Radiohead (and why you should too):

  • Thom Yorke's songwriting is legendary and unparalleled even after all these years
  • Each album has a unique theme
  • When the band tries to experiment with their sound (like in the album Amnesiac ) they do it well

((so please give them a try))
